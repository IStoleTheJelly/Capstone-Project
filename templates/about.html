About Sunrise Café

We’re a small, family-run café serving fresh coffee, flaky pastries, and simple, honest food. Our goal is to make mornings brighter—one cup, one smile at a time.

Who We Are

Sunrise Café started as a weekend pop-up with a borrowed espresso machine and a lot of heart. Today, we’re a local hangout where students, neighbors, and early-birds fuel up, catch up, and slow down for a minute. We keep things friendly, fast, and consistent.

What We Believe

Good coffee is simple, not complicated. We focus on quality ingredients, kind service, and a menu that’s easy to love. If it doesn’t taste great or make your day easier, it’s not on our menu.

  • Warm & welcoming: Everyone’s a regular here.
  • Consistent quality: If we can’t do it well, we don’t do it.
  • Time matters: Great coffee, without the wait.

What We Make

House drip, velvety lattes, seasonal specials, and cold brew on hot days. Fresh bakes every morning—croissants, muffins, banana bread—and quick bites like paninis and wraps.

  • Signature Latte (ask about the seasonal flavor!)
  • Cold Brew 16oz
  • Butter & Chocolate Croissants
  • Turkey Pesto & Caprese Paninis

How We Source

We partner with regional roasters and local bakeries for fresh products delivered daily. Our aim is simple—keep it local when possible and always keep it fresh.

Community & Sustainability

We donate unsold pastries to neighborhood mutual-aid fridges and offer a discount when you bring a reusable cup. Small steps add up.

What’s Next

We’re building an online ordering & inventory system to speed up pickups and keep popular items in stock. That means shorter lines, fewer phone orders, and more time for you.
